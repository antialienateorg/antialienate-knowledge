# EU Therapists & Clinicians — Parental Alienation Practitioner Directory

**Schema version:** 1.0
**Last updated:** 2026-05-27
**License:** CC BY 4.0 (intended for github.com/AntiAlienate/antialienate-knowledge)
**Region:** EU (DE / FR / ES / IT / NL / BE)
**Scope:** EU-member-state psychiatrists, psychologists (clinical / forensic / academic), psychotherapists and clinical social workers who have (a) worked PA cases as treating clinician or court-appointed expert, (b) published on PA in a peer-reviewed venue or recognised professional publication, or (c) publicly stated a sourced stance (recognition / critique / middle) on PA. Stance is sourced, not inferred.

**Standards:** Primary sources only — national register entries where searchable, practice / institutional website, peer-reviewed publications, court records, named press. Where a fact could not be confirmed from a public primary source it is flagged "**not publicly confirmed**".

**Stance taxonomy:**
- `recognition` — treats parental alienation / Eltern-Kind-Entfremdung / aliénation parentale / alienazione genitoriale / ouderverstoting as a real, assessable phenomenon; operates or endorses PA-specific assessment, expert-witness or reunification work; typically PASG-aligned.
- `critique` — argues the PA construct (especially Gardner's SAP) is operationalised against DV / coercive-control survivors (predominantly mothers and children); advocates DV/trauma-informed alternatives and tighter regulation of expert witnesses.
- `middle` — engages both alienating-behaviour and family-violence frames; differential-diagnosis or systemic / multi-family approach; not aligned with PASG and not in the abolitionist critique camp.

**Critical EU regulatory & policy context (must read before using this directory):**

- **Germany (DE):** Psychotherapists and physicians hold an **Approbation** (state licence) issued by the Land authority; psychotherapists must additionally be listed by the **Kassenärztliche Vereinigung (KV)** or the **Psychotherapeutenkammer** of the respective Land (e.g. https://www.lpk-bw.de/, https://www.ptk-berlin.de/). The 2023 **BVerfG order 1 BvR 1076/23** (case-file already in `/case-studies/`) is the most important German constitutional ruling on the use of "Eltern-Kind-Entfremdung" framing in custody transfers — every German recognition-camp expert below must be read against that ruling.
- **France (FR):** Psychiatrists are registered with the **Ordre national des médecins** (RPPS number, https://www.conseil-national.medecin.fr/annuaire); psychologists hold an **ADELI** number (now being merged into RPS at https://annuaire.sante.fr); court-appointed experts are listed on the **liste des experts judiciaires** of the relevant Cour d'appel (e.g. Versailles, Paris). The 2013 **Cass. 1re Civ. 12-14.392** (case-file already in `/case-studies/`) is the leading French civil ruling cautioning against routine "SAP" findings. The 26 December 2019 circular CRIM 2019-19/H2 of the Ministry of Justice on intra-family violence is the doctrinal anchor for the French critique camp.
- **Spain (ES):** Psychologists are registered with the **Colegio Oficial de Psicólogos** (COP) of the corresponding autonomous community (e.g. Asturias, Madrid, Cataluña); psychiatrists with the **Organización Médica Colegial (OMC)** provincial *Colegio de Médicos*. The **Ministry of Equality / Delegación del Gobierno contra la Violencia de Género** published the 2020 *Informe del Grupo de Trabajo sobre SAP* (https://violenciagenero.igualdad.gob.es/wp-content/uploads/ALIENACIONPARENTAL_cap2_lib7.pdf), which institutionally rejects SAP as a clinical construct. The 2017 **STS 519/2017** (case-file already in `/case-studies/`) is the leading Spanish Supreme Court ruling rejecting reliance on SAP without independent evidence.
- **Italy (IT):** Psychologists are on the **Ordine degli Psicologi** regional register (e.g. Ordine Lazio: https://ordinepsicologilazio.it/; Ordine Lombardia); psychiatrists on the **Federazione Nazionale degli Ordini dei Medici (FNOMCeO)**. The 2022 Cassazione **ord. 9691/2022 Massaro** and 2025 **ord. 4595/2025** (both already in `/case-studies/`) define the Italian doctrine: PA is not a clinically validated construct binding on judges and may not be used to disregard the child's stated views.
- **Netherlands (NL):** Psychologists are registered in the **BIG-register** (Wet BIG, https://www.bigregister.nl/) as *Klinisch psycholoog* or *Gezondheidszorgpsycholoog*; psychotherapists separately under Wet BIG. The leading Dutch Supreme Court decision **Hoge Raad 15 February 2005 ECLI:NL:HR:2005:AR8250** (case-file already in `/case-studies/`) on contact-withholding remains the doctrinal anchor.
- **Belgium (BE):** Psychologists are registered with the **Commission des psychologues / Psychologencommissie** (https://www.compsy.be/); clinical psychologists/orthopédagogues additionally with the relevant communauté health authority under the 2014/2016 reforms. There is **no Belgian appellate ruling that has fully endorsed SAP**; the dominant clinical doctrine is mixed.
