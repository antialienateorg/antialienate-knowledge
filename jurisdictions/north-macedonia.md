# North Macedonia (Северна Македонија)

**Jurisdiction code:** `MK` · **Legal system:** civil-law  
**Language(s):** mk, sq  

North Macedonia is a Western Balkans civil-law unitary republic whose family-law framework operates under the Закон за семејството (Law on the Family) of 1992 as substantially amended. Parental rights (родителски права) are governed by arts. 45-79; joint exercise during marriage is the statutory default. The Врховен суд (Supreme Court of North Macedonia, Skopje) is the apex court for civil and criminal matters; the Уставен суд (Constitutional Court) operates separate constitutional-review jurisdiction. Psychology profession is regulated under Ministry of Health licensing framework with the Комора на психолози на Република Северна Македонија (Chamber of Psychologists) operating professional standards. North Macedonia is silent on 'parental alienation' as a statutory label; courts operate substantively under the најдобар интерес на детето (best-interests-of-the-child) standard. Council of Europe member.
