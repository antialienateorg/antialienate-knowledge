# Ascension Island

**Jurisdiction code:** `SH-ASC` · **Legal system:** common-law  
**Language(s):** en  

Ascension Island is a South Atlantic common-law dependency of the British Overseas Territory of Saint Helena, Ascension and Tristan da Cunha — structurally distinctive globally as the only state-level entity with no permanent civilian population and explicitly prohibited from acquiring such (no right of abode is conferred on any person born on Ascension Island under the Ascension Island Constitution Order 2009), as the central location of strategically critical US Air Force Auxiliary Field at RAF Ascension Island (US Operating Location-Antigua + Tracking Station + NASA Tracking Station + Cable & Wireless undersea-cable hub + BBC World Service relay station), and as the only state-level entity simultaneously hosting major UK military, US military, NASA tracking, BBC World Service, and Cable & Wireless undersea-cable infrastructure under a single sub-jurisdictional framework. Ascension Island lies in the equatorial South Atlantic ~1,500 km east of South America (Brazil) and ~2,000 km west of Africa (Liberia), with Saint Helena ~1,300 km southeast. The transient population of ~800 comprises UK military and contractor personnel (Royal Air Force, US Air Force, NASA contractors, BBC contractors), all of whom hold transient-employment status with no right of abode. The Ascension Island Constitution Order 2009 establishes the constitutional framework with no permanent civilian rights to settle. Family-law framework operates under English law via the Saint Helena, Ascension and Tristan da Cunha Constitution Order 2009 with local Ascension Island Council Ordinances. Parental authority and child custody would in principle operate under English law adapted to Ascension Island circumstances — theoretical given absence of permanent civilian population. The Ascension Island Magistrate's Court is the first-instance court; the Supreme Court of Saint Helena, Ascension and Tristan da Cunha is the intermediate appellate court; final appellate jurisdiction lies with the UK Privy Council (Judicial Committee). Ascension Island is silent on 'parental alienation' as a statutory label. UK is a Hague Convention 1980 party (acceded 20 May 1986) — Ascension Island Hague applicability via UK territorial extension.

## Key developments

- **1815** — British military garrison established on Ascension Island in 1815 to prevent Napoleonic-French rescue of Napoleon (exiled to Saint Helena) — beginning of British sovereignty.
- **1922** — Ascension Island formally became a dependency of Saint Helena in 1922.
- **1956** — US Air Force tracking station and NASA tracking station established on Ascension Island via UK-US bilateral agreement — beginning of strategic US/NASA presence.
- **1982** — Ascension Island became central logistical hub for UK Operation Corporate during Falklands War 1982 — established Ascension Island's modern strategic significance.
- **1986** — Hague Convention 1980 territorial extension by UK to Ascension Island effective 1 August 1986.
- **2009** — UK Order in Council of 8 July 2009 establishing the consolidated constitutional framework for Saint Helena, Ascension and Tristan da Cunha with explicit Ascension Island no-right-of-abode framework.
