# Albania (Shqipëria)

**Jurisdiction code:** `AL` · **Legal system:** civil-law  
**Language(s):** sq  

Albania is a Western Balkans civil-law unitary republic whose family-law framework operates under the Family Code (Kodi i Familjes, Law 9062/2003) in force 21 December 2003. Parental responsibility (përgjegjësia prindërore) is governed by Kodi i Familjes arts. 215-255; joint exercise during marriage is the statutory default. The Gjykata e Lartë (High Court of Albania, Tirana) is the apex court for civil and criminal matters; the Gjykata Kushtetuese (Constitutional Court) operates separate constitutional-review and individual-constitutional-complaint jurisdiction. Psychology profession is regulated under Law 40/2016 (On the Profession of Psychologist) establishing the Urdhri i Psikologut (Order of Psychologists) — statutory professional-order regime. Albania is silent on 'parental alienation' as a statutory label; courts operate substantively under the interesi më i lartë i fëmijës (best-interests-of-the-child) standard. Council of Europe member; ECHR Strasbourg jurisprudence applies.

## Statutory framework

- **Kodi i Familjes (Law 9062/2003) arts. 215-255** — Family Code 2003 — Parental responsibility (2003) — <https://qbz.gov.al/>
  - Federal statute on family law in force 21 December 2003. Arts. 215-255 govern parental responsibility; joint exercise during marriage is the statutory default. Welfare standard codified.
- **Law 40/2016 — On the Profession of Psychologist** — Law on the Profession of Psychologist (2016) — <https://qbz.gov.al/>
  - Federal statute establishing the Urdhri i Psikologut (Order of Psychologists) as statutory professional-order regulator. Mandatory membership for practising psychologists.
