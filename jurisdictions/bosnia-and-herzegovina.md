# Bosnia and Herzegovina (Bosna i Hercegovina)

**Jurisdiction code:** `BA` · **Legal system:** civil-law  
**Language(s):** bs, hr, sr  

Bosnia and Herzegovina is a Western Balkans civil-law federal state operating a complex constitutional architecture established by the Dayton Peace Agreement 1995. Family law operates at entity level: the Federation of Bosnia and Herzegovina (FBiH) operates the Porodični zakon FBiH 2005; Republika Srpska (RS) operates the Porodični zakon RS 2002; the Brčko District operates a separate family-law framework. Parental rights are governed by the entity codes; joint exercise during marriage is the statutory default. The Sud Bosne i Hercegovine (Court of BiH) handles state-level matters; family-law cases are heard within the entity courts with apex appellate jurisdiction in the entity Supreme Courts and constitutional review in the Ustavni sud BiH (Constitutional Court of BiH). Psychology profession regulation operates at entity level under Ministry of Health licensing frameworks. BiH is silent on 'parental alienation' as a statutory label; courts operate substantively under the welfare-of-the-child standard. Council of Europe member.

## Key developments

- **1995** — General Framework Agreement for Peace in Bosnia and Herzegovina signed 14 December 1995. Established constitutional architecture with FBiH + RS entities and Brčko District. European Convention incorporated via Annex 4.
- **2002** — Family Code of Republika Srpska enacted.
- **2005** — Family Code of the Federation of Bosnia and Herzegovina enacted.

## Structural findings

- Bosnia and Herzegovina operates a structurally distinctive federal architecture within the corpus — Dayton-established two-entity-plus-district framework (FBiH + RS + Brčko District) with family law operating at entity level. Distinctive among Western Balkans jurisdictions for the level of constitutional fragmentation.
- European Convention incorporated via Annex 4 to the Dayton Peace Agreement — gives ECHR direct domestic-law standing. Constitutional Court individual-constitutional-complaint pathway operates engaging Convention rights including Article 8 positive-obligations framework.
- Psychology profession regulation operates at entity level — parallel regulatory frameworks for FBiH and RS.
