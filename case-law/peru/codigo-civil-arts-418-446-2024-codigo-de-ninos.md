## TL;DR

Peru's **Código Civil** arts. 418–446 governs *patria potestad*, supplemented by the **Código de los Niños y Adolescentes** (Law 27,337 of 2000, regularly amended) which is the principal child-welfare statute. The 2024 PA bill (Proyecto de Ley) is under congressional consideration following Brazilian + Mexican models. Hague 1980 (2001) + Hague 1996 (2017). Active diaspora corridors with USA, Chile, Argentina, Spain, Italy.

## Statutory framework

### Código Civil arts. 418-446
- Joint *patria potestad* during marriage
- Welfare-of-child standard for custody on divorce

### Código de los Niños y Adolescentes (Ley 27,337/2000)
- Comprehensive child-protection statute
- Best interest of child paramount
- Article 81: judicial custody determinations
- Article 84: contact regime framework

### 2024 PA bill (Proyecto de Ley)
- Draws on Brazilian Law 12.318/2010
- Would establish explicit PA framework with graduated remedies

## Corte Suprema jurisprudence

### Multiple post-2018 decisions citing PA in welfare analysis
- Recognition of *alienación parental* concept growing

## Hague + cross-border framework

- **Hague 1980**: signatory since 1 Aug 2001; **Ministerio de la Mujer y Poblaciones Vulnerables** is CA
- **Hague 1996**: signatory since 1 Sep 2018
- Active corridors: USA, Chile, Argentina, Spain, Italy, Japan

## Diaspora pattern

- **USA**: ~700k Peruvian-American
- **Chile, Argentina**: substantial regional migration
- **Spain, Italy, Japan**: substantial communities
