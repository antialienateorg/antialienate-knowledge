## TL;DR

Colombia operates a dual framework: **Código Civil** (arts. 253–262 on parental authority) + **Ley 1098 of 2006** (Código de la Infancia y Adolescencia, Children and Adolescents Code). 2024 saw introduction of a Brazilian-style **PA bill** (Proyecto de Ley) in Congress drawing on Law 12.318/2010. Hague 1980 (1995) + Hague 1996 (2007). Major diaspora corridors with USA, Spain, Venezuela, Ecuador, Chile, Mexico.

## Statutory framework

### Código Civil arts. 253–262
- *Patria potestad* (parental authority) framework
- Joint exercise during marriage
- Welfare-of-child standard

### Ley 1098/2006 — Código de la Infancia y Adolescencia
- **Art. 8**: best interest of child paramount
- **Art. 14**: child's right to know and be cared for by both parents
- **Art. 23**: parental responsibility (*responsabilidad parental*)
- **Art. 44 et seq.**: parental responsibilities and child rights
- *Custodia y cuidado personal* framework

### Ley 1564/2012 (Código General del Proceso)
- Procedural framework for family-court proceedings

## 2024 PA bill (Proyecto de Ley)

- Introduced in Congreso de la República 2024
- Draws on Brazilian Law 12.318/2010 framework
- Would establish explicit PA statutory definition + graduated remedies
- Status: under legislative consideration as of early 2026
- Cross-pollination from Brazilian + Argentine frameworks

## Corte Suprema de Justicia + Corte Constitucional jurisprudence

### Corte Constitucional T-008/2018
- Best-interest paramountcy
- Application to custody disputes

### Corte Suprema, Sala de Casación Civil (multiple recent decisions)
- Application of post-2006 framework
- Increasing recognition of *alienación parental* concept

## Hague + cross-border framework

- **Hague 1980**: signatory since 1 Mar 1996; **Instituto Colombiano de Bienestar Familiar (ICBF)** is CA
- **Hague 1996**: signatory since 1 Mar 2008
- Active corridors: USA (~3M Colombian-Americans), Venezuela (bilateral diaspora), Spain, Ecuador, Chile, Mexico

## Parental alienation recognition

- Welfare-of-child framework permits PA-evidence
- Corte Constitucional and Corte Suprema recognise *alienación parental* in custody analysis
- 2024 PA bill would formalize
- Colombian psychology and family-law literature growing on PA

## Diaspora pattern

- **USA**: ~3M Colombian-American
- **Venezuela**: historic bilateral mobility (recently disrupted)
- **Spain**: ~300k
- **Ecuador, Chile, Mexico**: substantial
- **Canada, Costa Rica, Panama**: growing communities
