# Dr. Demosthenes Lorandos, PhD, JD — Forensic Methodology

Dual-credentialed psychologist and attorney whose work has focused on getting PA expert evidence admitted under US Daubert standards. Co-editor with Bernet of the field's standard reference work.

## Key contributions

- ***Foundations of Parental Alienation*** (Charles C Thomas, 2020, co-edited with Bernet) — the multi-disciplinary standard reference.
- Multiple Daubert-style expert-evidence challenges and successful PA testimony admissions in US courts.
- Published practitioner guidance on the methodology required for PA expert evidence to survive admissibility motions.

## Where to find his work

- Personal site: https://www.lorandos.com/
- ResearchGate: https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Demosthenes-Lorandos
- Books via Charles C Thomas Publisher.

## Why he matters here

If your case involves expert PA testimony in a US court (or any Daubert-style jurisdiction), Lorandos's methodology guidance is the gold standard for getting the expert evidence admitted. Cite *Foundations* (2020) when the opposing side argues PA is unreliable science.
