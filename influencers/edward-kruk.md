# Dr. Edward Kruk, PhD — Equal-Parenting Framework

Social worker and academic who has built the empirical case for the equal-shared-parenting presumption as the post-separation default. Founding president of the International Council on Shared Parenting (ICSP).

## Key contributions

- ***The Equal Parent Presumption: Social Justice in the Legal Determination of Parenting After Divorce*** (McGill-Queen's University Press, 2013) — the foundational academic case for the 50/50 presumption.
- ***A Call for a Reasonable Application of Shared Parenting Presumptions*** — peer-reviewed contributions to the equal-parenting policy debate.
- **International Council on Shared Parenting (ICSP)** — multi-jurisdictional research and policy network promoting shared parenting as the post-separation default.
- Co-author with Harman & Hines on the Psychological Bulletin 2018 PA framing piece.

## Why he matters here

Kruk is the policy-level upstream. When the framing question is "what should the legal default be?" — his 2013 book is the standard academic answer. The ICSP also tracks shared-parenting legislative reform across jurisdictions.
