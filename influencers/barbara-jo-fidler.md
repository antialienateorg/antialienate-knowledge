# Barbara Jo Fidler, PhD, C.Psych.

Clinical-forensic psychologist. The clinical counterpart to Nick Bala.

## Why she matters here

Fidler co-authored the field's most balanced practitioner text — *Children Who Resist Postseparation Parental Contact* — which evaluators, judges, and lawyers across North America treat as the differential-diagnosis reference. She trains parenting coordinators and reunification therapists internationally. If a Canadian evaluator references the "differentiated approach" to resist-refuse cases, that's Fidler's framework.

## Credentials & affiliation

- PhD, registered clinical-forensic psychologist
- Accredited family mediator (Ontario)
- Private practice, Toronto
- Senior trainer, Association of Family and Conciliation Courts (AFCC)
- Co-recipient (with Bala) of AFCC's top award for promoting dialogue on PA

## Key contributions

- ***Children Who Resist Postseparation Parental Contact*** (Oxford, 2013) — with Nick Bala and Rachel Birnbaum.
- **"Children Resisting Postseparation Contact With a Parent: Concepts, Controversies, and Conundrums"**, *Family Court Review*, 2010.
- AFCC parenting-coordination training curricula.
