# Influencers — Researchers, Clinicians & Practitioners Shaping the PA Field

Reference pages for the people whose work parents, lawyers, and researchers in the parental-alienation space need to know. Profiles include credentials, key publications, where to find their work, and (where publicly listed) contact paths.

## Field-defining researchers & clinicians

- **[William Bernet, MD](william-bernet.md)** — Vanderbilt; founder of the Parental Alienation Study Group (PASG)
- **[Richard A. Warshak, PhD](richard-warshak.md)** — author of *Divorce Poison*; creator of Family Bridges
- **[Amy J. L. Baker, PhD](amy-baker.md)** — author of *Adult Children of Parental Alienation Syndrome*
- **[Bill Eddy, LCSW, Esq.](bill-eddy.md)** — co-founder, High Conflict Institute; BIFF method
- **[Jennifer Harman, PhD](jennifer-harman.md)** — Colorado State University; PA prevalence research

## Practitioners, expert witnesses & legal scholars

- **[Edward Kruk, PhD](edward-kruk.md)** — UBC; shared-parenting research; ICSP founder
- **[Karen Woodall](karen-woodall.md)** — Family Separation Clinic, UK
- **[Craig Childress, PsyD](craig-childress.md)** — Multi-Modal Family Intervention; attachment-based PA model
- **[Demosthenes Lorandos, JD, PhD](demosthenes-lorandos.md)** — expert witness; *Parental Alienation Handbook*
- **[J. Michael Bone, PhD](michael-bone.md)** — Florida-based PA expert witness; "Four Criteria" framework
- **[Linda J. Gottlieb, LMFT, LCSW-R](linda-gottlieb.md)** — Turning Points for Families (TPFF) intensive reunification

## Resist-refuse framework

- **[Joan B. Kelly, PhD](joan-kelly.md)** — co-author of the 2001 reformulation of PA
- **[Janet R. Johnston, PhD](janet-johnston.md)** — co-author of the resist-refuse framework; bridge between camps

## Critics — included for balance

PA is a contested field. The open-source ethos requires that critics be referenced alongside proponents so readers see the real debate.

- **[Joyanna Silberg, PhD](joyanna-silberg.md)** — Leadership Council on Child Abuse and Interpersonal Violence; primary US clinical critic of PA-driven custody decisions

## Legacy figures

- **Steven G. Miller, MD** (1949–2022) — Harvard Medical School faculty 30 years; seminal lecturer on clinical decision-making errors in PA evaluations. Archived talks at [lindagottlieb.com/steven-miller-parental-alienation](https://lindagottlieb.com).

## Honourable mention

- **Robert Geffner, PhD** — President Emeritus, [Institute on Violence, Abuse and Trauma](https://ivatcenters.org); leading critic of PA reunification programmes (2022 critique in *J. Family Trauma, Child Custody & Child Development*).
- **Benoît Van Dieren** — French-language PA-prevention work, Belgium (no current authoritative public site verified).
