# Bill Eddy, LCSW, Esq. — High-Conflict Coparenting Methodology

Family law attorney and licensed clinical social worker. Co-founded the High Conflict Institute. Developed the BIFF method and the broader "high-conflict personality" framework now widely used by family-court practitioners and coparenting coaches.

## Key contributions

- **BIFF Method** — Brief, Informative, Friendly, Firm. The communication framework for responding to high-conflict coparenting messages without escalating or creating evidence against yourself. See [/open-source/infographics/biff-method.png](../open-source/infographics/biff-method.png) and [/templates/coparenting-biff-responses.md](../templates/coparenting-biff-responses.md).
- ***BIFF: Quick Responses to High-Conflict People*** (Unhooked Books) — short, practical book.
- ***Splitting: Protecting Yourself While Divorcing Someone with Borderline or Narcissistic Personality Disorder*** (New Harbinger).
- High Conflict Institute training for family-court practitioners, parenting coordinators, and judges.

## Where to find his work

## Why he matters here

When the alienating parent's litigation tactic depends on you reacting to provocations, Eddy's BIFF framework is the operational counter-tactic. Coparenting apps (OurFamilyWizard, TalkingParents) are now structurally built around BIFF principles.
