# Asunción Tejedor Huerta, PhD

Forensic psychologist. The leading Spanish-language voice on PA as a form of child maltreatment.

## Why she matters here

Tejedor developed PIVIP (*Programa de Intervención para Víctimas de Interferencias Parentales*) — one of the few structured European intervention protocols. She is essential for Spanish-speaking families and for understanding how SAP/PA is framed in a jurisdiction (Spain) where the government has officially rejected the term but courts continue to cite it. The Spanish-speaking PA literature routes through her.

## Key contributions

- ***El síndrome de alienación parental: una forma de maltrato*** (EOS, 2006; updated 2015) — the Spanish-language reference text.
