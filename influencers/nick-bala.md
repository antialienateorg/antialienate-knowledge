# Nicholas (Nick) Bala, LLM

Canada's leading legal academic on family violence, alienation, and children-resisting-contact cases.

## Why he matters here

Bala writes for judges. His Canadian case-law studies (Bala/Hunt/McCarney 2009; Bala/Hunter 2017) are routinely cited in Canadian, UK, and Australian judgments. If you want to know how courts actually adjudicate PA cases — not just how clinicians theorise about them — start with Bala.

## Credentials & affiliation

- LLM (Harvard)
- William R. Lederman Distinguished Professor, Faculty of Law, Queen's University, Kingston, Ontario
- Co-editor (with Barbara Jo Fidler and Joan Kelly), 2020 *Family Court Review* special issue on PA

## Key contributions

- ***Children Who Resist Postseparation Parental Contact*** (Oxford, 2013) — with Barbara Jo Fidler and Rachel Birnbaum.
- **"Parental Alienation: Myths, Realities & Uncertainties"** (2010) — the standard primer.
- **2024 SSRN paper "Children Resisting Contact & Parental Alienation: Strategies for Lawyers"** — practitioner-facing strategy guide.
- Canadian case-law studies (Bala/Hunt/McCarney 2009; Bala/Hunter 2017).
